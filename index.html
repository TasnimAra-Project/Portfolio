This project changed everything for me. As someone who loves Bollywood movies (especially Shah Rukh Khan!), I wanted to explore how data could tell stories about my favorite actors.

Key Questions I Explored:

  • How does the genre of a movie influence its IMDB rating?
  • Are established actors linked to higher movie ratings over the years?

What I Discovered:

My analysis revealed fascinating trends in Bollywood actor performance from 2018-2023:

  • Shahid Kapoor: Strong recent performance with higher ratings in 2022-2023
  • Salman Khan: Fluctuating ratings, suggesting mixed audience reception

Deal Activity Prediction: From Unsupervised to GPT-Enhanced ML

Machine Learning & Business Intelligence Python, OpenAI GPT-4, XGBoost, Random Forest, Clustering, Feature Engineering

My biggest challenge yet: Predicting deal closure probability from an unlabeled dataset of wealth management activities. This project pushed me to innovate with GPT-4 labeling and advanced ML techniques.

The Core Challenge:

  • Unlabeled dataset with deal activities (emails, meetings, notes, calls)
  • Need to predict probability of deal closing
  • Traditional unsupervised methods only achieved 34% accuracy
  • Required innovative approach to create labeled training data

My Two-Pronged Approach:

Phase 1: Unsupervised Learning
  • KMeans Clustering: Extracted numeric features (total activities, engagement days)
  • Agglomerative Clustering: Applied hierarchical clustering with k=3
  • Results: KMeans: 29.8% agreement, Agglomerative: 34.2% agreement
  • Insight: Traditional clustering couldn't capture nuanced deal patterns
Phase 2: GPT-4 Enhanced Supervised Learning
  • Data Processing: Flattened nested JSON, created chronological summaries
  • GPT-4 Labeling: Generated "High/Medium/Low" labels with reasoning
  • Feature Engineering: Activity ratios, recency metrics, engagement density
  • Model Training: Random Forest vs XGBoost with SMOTE balancing

Key Breakthrough: GPT-4 labeling captured business context that pure statistical methods missed, leading to a 97% improvement in accuracy (34% → 67%).

Technical Achievements & Results:

My final model comparison revealed significant improvements:

  • XGBoost Performance: 67% accuracy (vs 34% unsupervised)
  • Random Forest: 64% accuracy with better medium-class recall
  • Feature Importance: Email activity ratios and engagement density were top predictors
  • Business Value: Model can now identify high-risk deals for proactive intervention

Innovation Skills: This project taught me how to creatively solve "impossible" problems by combining cutting-edge AI (GPT-4) with traditional ML techniques — a skill I now apply to other unstructured data challenges.

Social Justice Legal Resource Chatbot: Technology for Community Empowerment

AI Chatbot & Database Design ChatGPT API, SQL, Python, Database Design, Natural Language Processing

My most meaningful project yet: Building a chatbot that acts as a virtual social justice lawyer, connecting vulnerable communities with critical legal resources based on their location and specific needs.

The Mission:

  • Democratize access to legal information and resources
  • Provide immediate assistance for urgent legal situations
  • Bridge the gap between communities and legal aid organizations
  • Create a user-friendly interface for complex legal navigation

Technical Architecture & Implementation:

Database Design & Data Management
  • SQL Database: Comprehensive legal resource repository with location-based indexing
  • Resource Categories: Housing, employment, immigration, family law, civil rights
  • Geographic Integration: ZIP code-based resource matching and filtering
  • Contact Information: Phone numbers, addresses, service areas, eligibility criteria
AI Chatbot Development
  • ChatGPT Integration: Leveraging OpenAI's API for natural language understanding
  • Context Awareness: Understanding legal situations and user urgency levels
  • Resource Matching: Intelligent filtering based on location, issue type, and eligibility
  • Conversational Flow: Guided questioning to gather necessary information efficiently

Key Innovation: Combining location-based database design with AI-powered natural language processing to create an accessible, immediate legal resource finder for communities in crisis.

Real-World Impact & User Experience:

Users can now get immediate legal assistance through natural conversation:

  • Scenario Example: "I'm getting kicked out of my home" + ZIP code → immediate housing legal aid resources
  • Resource Matching: Location-specific organizations, contact information, and service details
  • Accessibility: No legal jargon, simple language, immediate response
  • Comprehensive Coverage: Multiple legal areas with detailed resource information

Social Impact Skills: This project demonstrates my ability to apply data science and AI to solve real humanitarian challenges — creating technology that serves communities rather than just businesses. It shows my commitment to using technical skills for social good.
